Free delivery

Learn new skills

We’ve teamed up with our friends at Sound Seafood to create weekly Fish Boxes, which will be delivered to your door. Each month we choose two fish species for the box which will serve two or four people and is available every week for the month. You won’t find lots of plastic wrapped ingredients in our boxes though. As you know, Mark likes to keep things simple, so you will no doubt have the ingredients for the recipes in your store cupboard already or they will be easy to pick up from your local shop, and Mark will always suggest options for substitutions.

Once you’ve placed your order you will receive the recipes and an exclusive link to watch the online tutorial with Mark to learn how to prepare this week’s fish, before creating a delicious starter and main course. As this is pre-recorded you can watch in advance or stop and start as much as you wish, cooking it at your own pace, rather than having to keep up with a live demo!

A great way to try new fish, learn some new skills, and cook along with Mark in his home.

Order your boxes online to get cooking at the weekend – boxes are delivered on Fridays with no delivery charge to anywhere in England and Wales, so place your orders before 6pm on a Tuesday and start sharpening those knives!
