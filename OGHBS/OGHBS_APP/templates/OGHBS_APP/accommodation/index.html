B&Bs, hotels and glamping

We are often asked for recommendations of where to stay in and around Lyme Regis, so here are just a few of the lovely hotels, holiday homes and B&Bs we like to recommend. They are either a walk or a short drive away from The Oyster & Fish House.

Dower House
Luxurious accommodation in Rousdon, 5 minutes drive from Lyme Regis. The perfect country retreat with a swimming pool.

Dorset House
Stunning guest house with locally sourced food and drink. Ten minutes walk from The Oyster & Fish House.

Stunning seafront suite of Bell Cliff B&B
The Bell Cliff House is situated in an elevated position, benefiting from panoramic views over the whole Lyme Bay and Charmouth Cliffs.

Hedgehog Corner
Short break self catering accommodations each with own hot tub, 5 minutes from Lyme Regis.

Lyme Bay Holidays
Beautiful cottages in Dorset and Devon.

Lyme Bay Retreats
The Retreat is walking distance to Lyme Regis gardens and beach.

Larch House
Walking distance to the gardens and shops, The luxurious Larch House is a brand-new ‘Grand Design’ that sleeps 6 guests.

Toad Hall Cottages
Holiday homes in Lyme Regis and further afield.

Lower Keats Glamping
20 minutes drive from the restaurant and ten minutes from Axminster train station.

The Cowshed
30 minutes drive from Lyme Regis, stunning 4 bedroom holiday home, sleeping 8, complete with hot tub. Dogs welcome.

Stay In Devon
A great range of self-catering accommodation across the Lyme Regis area.

Belmont House Beer
Adults only bed and breakfast or private apartment, set in the heart of the delightful fishing village of Beer.
