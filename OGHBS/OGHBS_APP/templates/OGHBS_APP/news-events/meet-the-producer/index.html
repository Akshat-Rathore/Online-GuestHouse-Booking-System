Meet the producer

An evening with The Pig and Castlewood Vineyard

In the summer of 2019 we collaborated with Robin Hutson of The Pig Hotels and Rob Corbett of Devonshire vineyard Castlewood to produce Devon Minnow, a delicious Bacchus wine, made just up the road from The Oyster & Fish House.

For one night only, Mark, Rob, Robin and The Pig’s Chef Director James Golding will come together to host a special evening celebrating the best of South West food and wine.

A collaborative menu from Mark and James will showcase signature dishes from The Pig Hotels and The Oyster & Fish House paired to Castlewood’s sparkling wines and the exclusive Devon Minnow.

To book your table email marketing@theoysterandfishhouse.co.uk

Ticket price £70 per person including menu and paired wines.
Full payment required upfront. Tickets are non-refundable and non-exchangeable. The event will start at 7pm and end at 10pm

*

Any additional food and drink ordered on the night will be charged at the current menu price plus a service charge of 12.5%

*Should the restaurant be forced to closed due to any new Government restrictions at the time of the event, a new date will be arranged.
