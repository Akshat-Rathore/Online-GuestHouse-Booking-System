Winter getaways for £80 per person

Dinner, Bed & Breakfast

This autumn we have teamed up with our friends at Dower House Hotel to offer a dinner, bed and breakfast package for only £80 per person per night*, offering the perfect escape to the coast.

If you are looking for a winter getaway on the Dorset coast, book a luxury stay at Dower House, just ten minutes’ drive away from Lyme Regis and enjoy a meal at The Oyster & Fish House*, with an additional 10% discount off your meal.

Eat, drink, stay

Luxurious Dorset accommodation

Surrounded by rolling hills and open fields, Dower House is set within 2 acres of gardens complete with heated swimming pool and ample parking.

This boutique hotel provides an unrivalled standard of rooms offering ten beautiful en-suite bedrooms, some of which are pet-friendly. Start your day with a delicious breakfast of locally sourced produce and enjoy a pre-dinner drink or nightcap in the bar.

To check availability and book, please contact Dower House on 01297 21047. The offer runs from November to end of March, Sunday to Thursday. Please note this offer cannot be booked with The Fish House team.

Closed dates: Please note Dower House will be closed until 10th December and also the first two weeks of January.

*£80 per person is based on double occupancy. Rooms will be charged at £160 per night including breakfast for 2 people at Dower House and a £50 voucher to dine at The Oyster & Fish House. Guests will also receive 10% off their bill at The Oyster & Fish House.

Offer is valid Sunday to Thursday November to March.

All bookings must be made by telephone on 01297 21047 quoting Fish House Winter Offer when enquiring. Your Fish House reservation will be made by the Dower House team at the time of booking.

Additional nights can be booked. If the extra nights are on a Friday or Saturday they will be charged at the standard room rate.

The Anning and French Lieutenants Suites are not included in this offer but can be booked for an additional cost. Please speak with The Dower House team if you wish to upgrade.
